Project #1: Side-Effects May Include...


This video isn't meant to invalidate the emotions of those who are suffering from depression, but rather to pin-point how antidepressants are marketed to the general public as any other over-the-counter drug with the exception of having very questionable side-effects.

Project #2: Bad Rap


This project was made with the intention to educate people about the pitbull problem and to help shatter the negative stereotype surrounding these dogs. In addition to pitbulls over-crowding shelters, their bad reputation is a contributing factor in their declining adoptions.
