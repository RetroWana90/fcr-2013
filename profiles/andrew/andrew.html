My Remix

"My Health" Remix By: Andrew Montoya as a part of Film/Code Remix class at BAVC 2013.


This remix shows the persuasiveness of snack and fast-food advertising. These advertisements show people eating in a way that depicts unhealthy eating habits without any concept of nutrition. The ads focus on gluttony and over eating as a means to sell their products. To create this remix, I searched for ads that show people eating snacks and fast food. Its my intention to make people aware of these unhealthy food choices.
