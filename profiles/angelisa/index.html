TLC's: Moms and Tiaras

It's difficult being a pageant queen but being a mother of these up and coming contest winners can be even harder. "Moms and Tiaras" provides a different insight behind these prepubescent beauty queens: the mothers from the TLC show "Toddlers and Tiaras."
